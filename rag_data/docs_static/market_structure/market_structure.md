# Market Structure Reference

## SPY Futures (ES/MES) Overview

ES (E-mini S&P 500) and MES (Micro E-mini S&P 500) are futures contracts that track the S&P 500 index.

### Contract Specifications
- **ES Point Value:** $50 per point
- **MES Point Value:** $5 per point (1/10th of ES)
- **Tick Size:** 0.25 points
- **Tick Value:** $12.50 (ES), $1.25 (MES)

### Trading Sessions
- **Globex Session:** Sunday 6:00 PM - Friday 5:00 PM ET (with 1-hour daily break)
- **RTH (Regular Trading Hours):** 9:30 AM - 4:00 PM ET
- **Most Liquid Hours:** 9:30 AM - 11:30 AM ET and 3:00 PM - 4:00 PM ET

## Key Levels

### Previous Day Levels
- **PDH (Previous Day High):** Strong resistance level
- **PDL (Previous Day Low):** Strong support level
- **PDC (Previous Day Close):** Pivot reference point

### Weekly/Monthly Levels
- **Weekly High/Low:** Major support/resistance zones
- **Monthly High/Low:** Even stronger S/R zones
- **Quarterly Levels:** Institutional reference points

### Pivot Points
- **Pivot (P):** (PDH + PDL + PDC) / 3
- **R1:** (2 × P) - PDL
- **S1:** (2 × P) - PDH
- **R2:** P + (PDH - PDL)
- **S2:** P - (PDH - PDL)

## Market Conditions

### Trend States
1. **UPTREND:** Price > EMA9 > EMA20 > EMA50
2. **DOWNTREND:** Price < EMA9 < EMA20 < EMA50
3. **CHOP/SIDEWAYS:** EMAs tangled, no clear direction

### Volatility Regimes
1. **HIGH:** ATR > 1.3x 20-day average ATR
2. **MEDIUM:** ATR between 0.7x and 1.3x average
3. **LOW:** ATR < 0.7x average

### Time-Based Patterns
- **Opening Range (9:30-10:00):** High volatility, establish range
- **Mid-Morning (10:00-11:30):** Trend continuation/reversal
- **Lunch (11:30-1:30):** Low volume, choppy
- **Afternoon (1:30-3:00):** Position building
- **Close (3:00-4:00):** High volume, trend decisions

## Support/Resistance Behavior

### Level Interactions
1. **Breakout:** Price closes above/below level with momentum
2. **Rejection:** Price touches level and reverses
3. **Retest:** Price returns to broken level before continuing

### Trading Rules
- **Buy near support (PDL):** When trend is bullish
- **Sell near resistance (PDH):** When trend is bearish
- **Avoid entries:** In the middle of range (no edge)

## Common Patterns

### Continuation Patterns
- Higher lows in uptrend
- Lower highs in downtrend
- Pullbacks to EMAs

### Reversal Patterns
- Double top/bottom at key levels
- Failed breakouts
- Divergences (price vs RSI/MACD)
